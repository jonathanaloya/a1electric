Ordering & delivery

From enquiry to delivery — how an A1 Electricals order moves from our showroom to your site.

Fast quotations Showroom pickup Safe delivery
01

Enquiry & quotation

Send a product list or photo over WhatsApp, phone or in person at our showroom. We confirm stock and pricing, typically the same day.

02

Order confirmation

On agreeing your order and payment (cash, mobile money or bank transfer), items are reserved from stock or ordered in from the manufacturer.

03

Preparation

Multi-item orders are picked and prepared at our Kampala showroom ahead of collection or delivery.

04

Delivery or collection

Collect from our showroom, or we arrange delivery across Kampala and wider Uganda for larger orders.

05

Delivery confirmation

We confirm by WhatsApp once your order is on its way, and again once it's safely arrived.

Collection

Pick up from our showroom

Customers in Kampala can collect ready orders directly from our office, with our team helping you confirm the right products and quantities before you leave.

Delivery

Reliable transfer for larger orders

We arrange delivery for bigger jobs and project orders, helping keep your materials moving safely and on schedule across Kampala and beyond.

Payment

Simple ways to pay

We accept cash, mobile money and bank transfer, and can discuss credit terms for established business accounts after review.
